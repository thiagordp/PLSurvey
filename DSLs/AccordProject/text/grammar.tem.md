# Data Use Contract

Art.1. Parties and Scope
This Agreement is concluded between {{user}} (User) and {{dataHolder}} (Data Holder) and governs the access to and use of all data generated by the vehicles purchased by the User from the Data Holder (the Product). 

For the purposes of this Agreement, “Data” refers to {{dataDescription}}.

Art. 2 Data Access Right
Upon request by the User, the Data Holder shall make the Data and necessary metadata accessible to the User or a third party acting on their behalf within {{deadlineDays}}.

Art.3 User Permitted Uses
The User may use the Data made available by the Data Holder upon their request for any lawful purpose and/or share the Data freely subject to the limitations below. The User shall not share the Data with any third party that qualifies as a Gatekeeper under Article 3 of Regulation (EU) 2022/1925 (Digital Markets Act), unless explicitly authorized in writing by the Data Holder.
